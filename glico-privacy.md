# Privacy Policy for Glico

*Last updated: May 15, 2026*

## Introduction

Francesco Bianco ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Glico mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data — including sensitive health data — when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## Information We Collect

### Health Data You Enter
- **Blood Glucose Readings**: values you log manually, including measurement context (fasting, post-meal, etc.), notes, and timestamps
- **Target Ranges**: your custom target values per measurement context
- **App Settings**: your preferences for units (mg/dL or mmol/L), notifications, and app behavior

### Apple Health (HealthKit)
- If you enable the Apple Health integration, Glico may **read** blood glucose data already stored in Health and **write** new readings back to Health
- This data is stored exclusively in your Apple Health database and is governed by Apple's privacy policies
- We do not access, transmit, or store HealthKit data on external servers

### Data We Don't Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information such as your name, email, or contact details
- We do **not** access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** sell your data to third parties

## How We Use Your Data

### Local Storage
- All blood glucose readings and settings are stored **locally on your device** using SwiftData
- Your health data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
- If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in **your private iCloud account** via CloudKit
- We cannot access your iCloud data — it is end-to-end encrypted by Apple
- You can disable sync at any time from within the app

### Notifications
- If you enable reminders, Glico uses iOS local notifications — no data is transmitted externally
- Notification preferences are stored locally on your device

### Analytics
- We may collect **anonymous, aggregated usage statistics** (e.g. feature usage, crash reports) to improve the app
- No health data or personal information is included in analytics

## HealthKit Data Usage

Glico uses HealthKit solely to provide the blood glucose tracking features described in the app. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than the features you explicitly activate. Health data is never shared with third parties.

## Data Security

- All local data is protected by your device's security features (Face ID / Touch ID, device passcode)
- iCloud data is encrypted using Apple's industry-standard security systems
- We do not operate external servers that store your health data

## Your Rights

### Data Control
- You have full control over your blood glucose data
- You can export your data as CSV directly from the app
- You can delete all your data by removing the app, or by clearing your iCloud data from Settings

### Analytics Opt-Out
- You can disable analytics in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements

## Children's Privacy

Glico is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Medical Disclaimer

Glico is a **data logging tool only**. It does not provide medical advice, diagnose medical conditions, or replace your doctor or healthcare professional. Always consult a qualified healthcare specialist for any health-related decisions.

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
- **HealthKit**: For reading and writing blood glucose data to Apple Health (your private account)
- **iCloud / CloudKit**: For optional data synchronization (your private iCloud account)
- **App Store**: For app distribution and in-app purchase management
- **WidgetKit**: For displaying glucose data in home screen and lock screen widgets

## International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained on your device until you delete the app or clear the data
- **iCloud Data**: Retained in your private iCloud account until you disable sync or delete the app
- **Analytics**: Anonymous, aggregated data retained for up to 24 months
